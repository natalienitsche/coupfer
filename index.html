COUPFER

~ Couples and Childbearing: New Approaches to the Study of Fertility Outcomes and Family Formation Across Europe ~

COUPFER is a research project funded by the European Commission with a Marie Curie International Incoming Fellowship. The aim of the project is to investigate how relative resources in couples are related to union stability and first, second, and third births across Europe.
